### Time complexities of forward_list functions

+ `insert_after()` - O(1) for 1 item. O(m) for m items.
+ `erase_after()`  - O(1) for 1 item. O(m) for m items.
+ `push_front()`   - O(1)
+ `pop_front()`    - O(1)
+ `reverse()`      - O(n)
+ `sort()`         - O(n(log n))
+ `remove()`       - O(n)
+ `assign()`       - O(1) for 1 item. O(m) for m items.
